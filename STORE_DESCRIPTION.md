## App Name
KeyLoom

## Subtitle (optional)
Floating Virtual Keyboard for macOS

## Description

KeyLoom is a native macOS floating virtual keyboard that lives above all windows, accessible from the menu bar. Click keys to type directly into any application - perfect for broken keyboards, testing, accessibility needs, or when you prefer a visual typing interface.

### Type Anywhere
Click any key on the virtual QWERTY layout and KeyLoom pastes it into your active app using Cmd+V. No dragging, no switching windows - just point, click, and type.

### Clipboard History
KeyLoom automatically monitors your clipboard and stores up to 1,000 items. Search, pin favorites (up to 5), and paste with a single click. Export your clipboard history to a text file anytime.

### Quick Keys Panel
Minimize the full keyboard into a compact strip of your 2-5 most-used keys. Custom keys auto-select based on usage, or hand-pick your own. Perfect for one-handed or repetitive typing.

### Customize Everything
- **Layout**: Adjust key size, spacing, corner radius, font size, and keyboard width
- **Appearance**: Choose from 8 fonts (Rounded, System, Mono, Georgia, Courier, Zapfino, Papyrus, Marker Felt), enable neomorphism 3D effects, adjust opacity and shadows
- **Broken Keys**: Highlight frequently used or broken keys in Blue, Red, Orange, or Purple
- **Sound**: 6 distinct key click sounds (Key Click, Soft, Mechanical, Glass, Minimal, Bottle) with adjustable volume
- **Behavior**: Launch at login, start in expanded or quick keys mode, toggle decorative keys

### Keyboard Sync
Your physical Shift and Caps Lock states sync in real time with the virtual keyboard. Hold Shift on your physical keyboard to temporarily shift, or press Caps Lock to lock caps mode on.

### Works Everywhere
- Floats above all windows and full-screen apps
- Works across all virtual desktops (Spaces)
- Draggable by window background
- Lives in your menu bar - accessible with one click

### Privacy First
KeyLoom runs entirely on your machine. Clipboard history is stored locally on disk. No data is sent anywhere. No tracking. No accounts.

### Requirements
- macOS 13 Ventura or later
- Accessibility permission required for automatic paste
